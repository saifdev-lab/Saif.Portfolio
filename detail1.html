LONG STORY SHORT

I initiated, designed, and built "IDN CAMP WEBSITE" from scratch as an individual project.

I designed and built this website as a personal portfolio case study to explore how an educational concept can be translated into a clear, engaging, and well-structured digital experience.

The project presents a learning program that blends technology education with collaborative activities. The focus was on communicating the idea through a clean layout and clear information hierarchy.

I was responsible for the entire process, starting from defining the concept to implementing the website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

MY GOALS

Based on my learning goals and design exploration objectives.

Clear Concept

Transforming an educational idea into a structured and intuitive digital experience.

User-Centered

Designing content hierarchy layouts that guide users naturally without cognitive overload.

End-to-End Execution

Owning the entire process from concept definition to front-end implementation.

UI Quality Focus

Applying attention to spacing, typography, and visual balance for a consistent interface.
