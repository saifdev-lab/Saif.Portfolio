I'm Saif Muhammad Yusuf — I design to inspire learning and craft meaningful digital experiences.

Growing up in the collaborative and innovative environment of IDN Boarding School, Bogor, West Java, I developed a deep curiosity for how people interact with technology and learn through design. Every individual has a unique learning journey — yet many share the same challenges in staying engaged, motivated, and inspired.

Throughout my years at IDN, I explored how design can simplify complex ideas and make education more accessible. From experimenting with interface layouts to developing interactive learning dashboards, my work is driven by a passion to empower students through thoughtful and intuitive digital experiences.

In addition to UI/UX, I also specialize in motion design and 3D design, bringing ideas to life through dynamic visuals and immersive interactions that enhance the overall learning experience.
